Warning!

Germs are spread by coughing, sneezing and spitting. This may cause harm to your customers and other staff.

You need to let staff know the risks and legal requirements. You can give staff fact sheets at the end of the topic or you may choose to get them to complete the free online training program DoFoodSafely .

We recommend you keep a list of the training that you give to staff. A template log is included in the resources to help you.
