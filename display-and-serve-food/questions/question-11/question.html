Single use items include:
  • plastic drinking straws
  • plastic cutlery
  • single serve sachets or packets of food such as sugar, salt, pepper or sauce
Some ways to protect single serve items include:
  • keep items away from animals, pests and children
  • only place small quantities on display at a time
  • throw away all items after one use
  • throw away items that have been handled by a customer
  • throw away items that have been dropped on the floor
  • throw away items where the outer protective packaging has been damaged.
