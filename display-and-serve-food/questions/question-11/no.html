Warning!

It is important to protect single use items from contamination. Single use items must only be used once. Single use items are items that cannot be cleaned and sanitised.

Single use items must be thrown out:

  • after they have been used
  • if they become contaminated
  • if you think that they may be contaminated.

Customers may choose to re-use single use items. For example, by re-using a takeaway container at home. Single use items cannot be re-used by a food business.
