Warning!

You should limit the time that you display and keep food in the temperature danger zone. This will help to stop bacteria and toxins from growing in your food.

You must be able to show that you keep food for no more than 4 hours in the temperature danger zone.

The 2-hour/4-hour rule helps you to show that your food is safe. See the fact sheet and template record at the end of this topic for more information on the 2-hour/4-hour rule.
