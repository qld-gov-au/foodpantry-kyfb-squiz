Warning!

Your food may not be safe as these types of equipment are designed to keep food warm, not to heat food.

You should not use the bain-marie or pie warmer to heat or re-heat food.

We recommend that you:

  • Always heat or reheat the food in the microwave, oven or stove top.
  • Place the food in the bain-marie or pie warmer to keep it hot.
