Warning!

You must supervise self-serve foods so that you can quickly remove contaminated food.

For example, remove food that:

  • a customer has touched.
  • a customer has sneezed or coughed on
  • a customer has taken and put back
  • a customer has cross contaminated one food with another food

The level of supervision will depend on the:

  • type of food displayed
  • the number of customers that have access the food
  • how accessible the food is.

You must provide separate serving utensils for each food item to prevent cross contamination. The handles must not come in contact with the food.
