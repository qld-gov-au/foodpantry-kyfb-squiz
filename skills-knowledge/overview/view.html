Skills and knowledge

Everyone working in a food business must have some skills and knowledge to handle food safely.

The level of food safety skills needed, will depend on the tasks done. For example, a chef that prepares food will need to know more about food safety than a shop assistant who restocks the refrigerator.

Before you start

To complete this topic, you should think about:

  • the types of food you handle
  • the level of food processing you undertake
  • training of your food safety supervisor and food handlers
