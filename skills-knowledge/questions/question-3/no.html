Warning!

Food safety training for staff will reduce the risk of food safety issues. Training could include:

  • induction training for new staff
  • on the job training
  • having staff complete free online courses
  • hiring a consultant to provide specialised training for your business

We recommend you maintain food safety training records for all food handlers at your food business. This includes:

  • internal training (e.g.: induction training)
  • on the job training
  • external training courses that provides a certificate of completion.
