Cleaning and sanitising play a very important role in making sure you produce safe food. Sanitising can be done two ways, with heat or by using a food grade sanitiser.

There are 6 easy steps to clean and sanitise dishes that are hand washed

  1. Pre-clean: scrape or wipe away food scraps and other matter and rinse with water.
  2. Wash: use hot water and detergent to take off any grease and dirt. Soak if needed.
  3. Rinse: rinse off detergent and any loosened residue.
  4. Sanitise: use a sanitiser to destroy any remaining bacteria.
  5. Final Rinse: wash off sanitiser if necessary (refer to manufacturerâ€™s instructions).
  6. Dry: allow to drip dry or dry using single use towels.

Dishes can be sanitised using a commercial dishwasher where the temperature reaches greater than 80 degrees.
