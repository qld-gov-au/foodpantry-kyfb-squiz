Animals and pests

Animals and pests can:

  • contaminate surfaces, equipment and food
  • transfer bacteria from their bodies onto anything they touch.

Guide dogs and other assistance animals are allowed in any area used by customers. Assistance animals are defined by the Disability Discrimination Act 1992 (Cth).

Only dogs are allowed in outdoor dining areas if you as the business choose to allow them. Check with your local council for the rules that apply in your area.

Before you start

To complete this topic you will need to think about how you:

  • prevent pests
  • manage animals at your food business
