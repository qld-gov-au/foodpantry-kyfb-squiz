Warning!

It is important that you get food label information from your supplier for each item of food you use.

You can find label information:

  • on the food package
  • attached to the food package
  • in documentation provided by the supplier.

Food labelling information will help you to:

  • Give information to your customers, so they can make informed choices based on their dietary needs (e.g.: for an allergy).
  • Manage your stock to use food within its expiry date.
  • Identify food that is part of a food recall.
