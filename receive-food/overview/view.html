Receive Food

Food businesses must only receive food that is safe and suitable. This means that food delivered to your business:

  • is protected from contamination
  • is labelled correctly
  • is at the correct temperature when it arrives.

Before you start

To complete this topic, you should think about:

  • the type of food you receive
  • checks you do when you receive a delivery
  • where is food is delivered and stored until it is checked?
  • what time and day of the week that is food delivered?
  • who are your suppliers?
