Warning!

Recalled food is unsafe to use and sell. Information on what to do with the recalled food is in the recall notice.

We recommend you have a process in place to:

  • check for food recalls
  • isolate recalled food before you are able to dispose or return the food.

You must keep the recalled food separate and clearly marked until you can:

  • return the food to the supplier
  • dispose of the food.

A separate storage area is not required but a clearly, labelled shelf or container is helpful.

We recommend that you train staff in your food recall process so that they do not accidentally sell or use any recalled food.
