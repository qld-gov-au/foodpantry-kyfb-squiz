Warning!

Cross contamination is one of the main causes of food poisoning and allergic reactions.

We recommend you reduce the risk of harming a customer by:

  • having processes to prevent cross contamination of food
  • training staff in how to do these processes
  • training staff so that they understand why they must follow the processes.

At the end of this topic, you can find:

  • fact sheets and posters on cross contamination
  • a guide on how to develop a cleaning roster with a template to record cleaning.
