Warning!

Your food may be taking too long to cool down. This can allow food poisoning bacteria or toxins to grow in your food.

You can reduce the time it takes to for food to cool by:

  • cooking smaller portions
  • separating batches into smaller, shallow containers.

See the factsheet below for more information on cooling food safety. To show you are cooling food safely, you can choose to keep records like the template record sheet at the end of the topic.
