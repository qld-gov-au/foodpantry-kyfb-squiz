Transport food

You must transport food in a way to keep the food safe. Transport includes moving food from one place to another:

  • within the same premises (e.g. between wards in an aged card facility)
  • to another premises (e.g.: from your kitchen to a market stall or customerâ€™s home)

You will need to plan how and when you transport your food, so that food is not contaminated during delivery. Your plan will need to consider:

  • delivery and dispatch times
  • the vehicle used to deliver the food
  • access to premises
  • a suitable place for the food to be loaded and unloaded.
