Warning!

Your food may be unsafe and may cause food poisoning.

It is important to only keep high risk foods for a short time. Some bacteria can grow even when the food is refrigerated.

We recommend that you:

  • Make a fresh batch of high risk food each day.
  • Throw out high risk foods at the end of each day.
  • Label foods with a date that it was opened or made, so that you can keep track of how long each item has been stored for.
  • Follow the storage instructions from your supplier.
