Warning!

You must prevent single use items from being contaminated while being stored. This includes single use items that customers can access (e.g.: straws).

Some ways to protect single use items from contamination include:

  • store off the floor
  • store in protective packaging or containers
  • store away from chemicals
  • store away from animals, pests and children
  • store ready to use cups, containers and cutlery upside down.
