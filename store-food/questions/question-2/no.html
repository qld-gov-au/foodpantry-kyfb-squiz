Warning!

You must protect your food from contamination.

We recommend you:

  • Have regular pest control done and keep receipts for each time it is done.
  • Regularly check for signs of pests (e.g.: mouse poo, spider webs). Call your pest control technician if you need extra treatment.
  • Check for pest entry points (e.g.: holes in walls, gaps under doors).

The factsheet at the end of the topic has easy steps to help you store your food safely.
